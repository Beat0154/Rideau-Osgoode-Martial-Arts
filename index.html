    Rideau Osgoode Karate Club

    Rideau Osgoode Martial Arts has served the community since 1991. We offer a family-friendly environment, with classes in traditional Karate, JiuJitsu, and Kobudo for members both young and old.

    Authentic Traditional Martial Arts. Karate, JiuJitsu, Kobudo. Flexible schedule. Affordable prices. Knowledgeable instructors. Fun and friendly environment. Classes for all ages.

    Beginners are always welcome.

    Located just south of Ottawa, the National Capital of Canada, Rideau Osgoode Martial Arts services the township of Osgoode with members from Metcalfe, Manotick, Greely, Mountain, North Gower, Osgoode, Kars, Kemptville, Heckston, and Ottawa.
